Dream Travel Itinerary


First part

-Our dream vacation would be full of trips to different places of the world, like to Machu Picchu in Peru, Time Square and Las Vegas in The United States, the pyramids of Egypt where is possible discover the secrets they keep.


Second part

-Maybe some countries in Europe to see the beauties, the landscapes and the rich culture of these countries.


Third part

-On our dream vacation we would also like to take a cruise along the crystal clear beaches of the Caribbean where we could rest a lot at the resorts to forget our troubles.


Fourth part

-We also think about getting away from the city and going to nature to breathe some fresh air and go camping and hiking together with friends.


Fifth part

-We would like to attend concerts at Music Festivals such as Pink Pop, which brings together several bands from the international scene, such as Cold Play, Pearl Jam, Green Day, among others.


Sixth part

- And to finish, spend some time at home watching some series and preparing ourselves to get back to routine.


Travel tips and ideas:

Adventure

Climbing the grand canyon in the USA, taking a walk through the desert in Egypt and being dazzled by the pyramids.

Enthusiasm

A valuable tip is to venture into cycling through some cities in Europe and be enchanted by the various landscapes there.

Discovery

In Europe there are several historical monuments and fantastic works to be visited, even more several museums can be visited.
